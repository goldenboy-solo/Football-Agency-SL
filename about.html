About Us

Our Vision

To become the leading football agency in West Africa, discovering and nurturing Sierra Leone's exceptional football talent and connecting them with opportunities on the global stage. We envision a future where Sierra Leonean players are recognized worldwide for their skill, dedication, and sportsmanship.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing comprehensive player representation, career guidance, and development opportunities for Sierra Leone's most promising football talents. Our mission is to bridge the gap between local talent and international football markets while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and ethical practice.

Our History

Founded in 2022, Football Agency SL was established by a group of passionate football enthusiasts from Limkokwing University. Since our inception, we have successfully represented over 50 players and facilitated numerous international transfers. Our track record speaks to our dedication and expertise in player management.

Our Team

Management Team

Our experienced management team brings decades of combined experience in football administration, player representation, and sports law. We work tirelessly to ensure our players receive the best opportunities and support throughout their careers.

  • Expert negotiators with international club contacts
  • Licensed FIFA intermediaries
  • Former professional players and coaches

Scouting Network

With scouts across Sierra Leone, we maintain a comprehensive network that identifies emerging talent from youth leagues to professional divisions. Our scouts are trained to recognize not just skill, but also character and potential.

  • Nationwide talent identification system
  • Regular youth tournaments and trials
  • Performance tracking and analytics

Support Staff

From career advisors to legal consultants, our support staff ensures that every aspect of a player's professional life is handled with care and expertise. We provide holistic support that goes beyond the pitch.

  • Legal and contract specialists
  • Sports psychologists and nutritionists
  • Media training and brand management
